About the Company
they started their business by selling fishballs and kikiam, until they developed many different types of dishes that they wanted to sell such as Mami, Tapsilog, Palabok, and Lomi until the number of customers increased and the main star dish that people keep coming back to is their Lomi and this is the reason why he called his business Kuya Ernesto Lomihan.
compared to before, their business has come a long way and according to Mr. Ernesto"before you reach the thing you really want you will go through many trials and you have to be hands on, be patient in your business" and the main purpose of Mr. Ernesto's business is to support the daily needs of his family and to become a Financially stable

History

Kuya Erning Lomihan started when Mr.Ernesto stopped being an Over seas worker. He said that he don't want to go abroad anymore and he would just set up a business to support his family's needs. Kuya Ernings Lomihan started before the pandemic they started their business by selling fishballs and kikiam, until they caught the taste of their customers and they developed many different types of dishes that they wanted to sell such as Mami, Tapsilog, Palabok, and Lomi until the number of customers increased.

Mission

the mission of their business is To understand our customer’s changing needs and constantly improve our customer experience and To delight and nourish their customers with quality and delicious food and excellent service at a reasonable price.


Vision

To serve happiness to our customers through delicious and quality meals while working toward the greater good for our community and environment.


Nature of Business

This business is a soleproprietorship and this is a Foods business that sell foods to satisfy their customer needs and wants


Governance

They don't have much of a policy when it comes to their business unlike other food businesses they have a policy that if you don't finish your food, you will pay money depending on the food you have left. but even if they don’t have a policy they make sure that the meals served that day at their business are always fresh. Mr. Ernesto makes sure that they buy fresh ingredients for their business every day and they also cook it on the day when it is purchased.


News & Events

Before and Post Pandemic Events they used to only have a dine-in services before the pandemic and they had a lot of customers but when the pandemic came they didn't close but because of the pandemic they adapted and because of that they innovated their business by having a delivery services they posted their dishes online and then the customer will message the page and they will deliver it to your house and the mode of payment is Cash on Delivery but even though they have that kind of innovation in their business the sales of their business has weakened due to the pandemic but they still continue to sell through online and provide delivery services even though that is what happened to their business


Highlights

the highlights of their business is when pandemics came to the philippines, when the pandemic came into the philippines many establishments were forced to close due to the pandemic, but Mr. Ernesto's business did not close but they innovated their business by having a delivery services and facebook page where they will post their dishes online and people will message their page and Mr.Ernesto Itself will deliver to their house and the mode of payment is cash on delivery
