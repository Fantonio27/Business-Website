Overview

Before opening a small restaurant, Mr. Ernesto claims that they began selling a variety of street foods such as pika-pika, squid balls, kikiam, and so on. Following that, they experiment with selling and creating various types of food that Filipinos will seek out and crave. They occasionally improve the food they sell based on customer feedback and suggestions. Mr. Ernesto stated that looking back from where they started today, they have come a long way toward achieving what they have achieved.

Our Products

Kuya Erning's Pancit Palabok is a delicious Filipino noodle dish topped with shrimp gravy, shrimp, smoked fish flakes, pork cracklings, and eggs. It's filling and savory, and it's perfect for a light meal or anytime snack. Another example of the Filipinos' fondness for noodle dishes is pancit palabok.

Silog Teriyaki: Kuya Erning's silog teriyaki consists of deep fry chicken pieces brushed with soy sauce, mirin, sugar, and ginger sauce. The most important aspect of teriyaki is the caramelization on the outside of the meat. That's what makes it so tasty.

Special Lomi: Kuya Erning's Special Lomi is a unique Filipino noodle soup. Kuya Erning's Special Lomi is made with thick noodles and flavored thick soup, as well as a chicharron, a slick boiled egg, and meat.
